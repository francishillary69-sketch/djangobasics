About Salama Sales Ltd

Salama Sales Ltd is a leading automotive company specializing in vehicle sales, spare parts distribution, and professional repair services. Founded on the principles of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation as a trusted partner in the automotive industry.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive automotive solutions including:

  • Vehicle Sales: Quality used and new vehicles carefully inspected and maintained
  • Spare Parts Distribution: Genuine and aftermarket parts from trusted manufacturers worldwide
  • Professional Repair Services: Expert mechanical repairs and maintenance by certified technicians
  • Consultation: Professional advice on vehicle selection, maintenance, and repairs

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading automotive provider in the region, delivering exceptional quality vehicles, parts, and repair services with a commitment to customer satisfaction and affordability.

Our Vision

To empower vehicle owners with access to quality automobiles, reliable spare parts, and expert repair services at competitive prices.

  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our top priority
